# SciLens — Chemicals Scoring Guide

## Scope
Industrial chemistry, process safety, green chemistry, REACH, EPA TSCA, materials science, catalysis.

## TRL Calibration
| Paper evidence | TRL |
|---------------|-----|
| Molecular design, theoretical | 1–2 |
| Lab synthesis, characterization | 3 |
| Lab-scale process, reaction optimization | 4 |
| Pilot-scale demonstration | 5–6 |
| Commercial-scale with safety validation | 7–9 |

## Evidence Quality Priority
Pilot/commercial demo > lab-scale with reproducibility > computational/screening

## Key Analysis Focus
- REACH (EU): registration, evaluation, authorization requirements
- EPA TSCA (US): new chemical substance review
- GHS classification: hazard communication requirements
- Process safety: HAZOP, LOPA, inherently safer design
- Green chemistry metrics: atom economy, E-factor, PMI, solvent selection guide
- Scale-up pathway from batch to continuous/flow chemistry

## Market Context Instructions
Chemical market validation comes from industrial adoption, not clinical trials.
Citation data is often a stronger signal than trial counts for this domain.
Reference regulatory registrations as market access signals.

## Risk Factors to Prioritize
1. REACH/TSCA registration cost and timeline
2. Hazard classification impact on handling/transport
3. Scale-up safety risks (exotherms, runaway reactions)
4. Raw material availability and price volatility
5. Green chemistry compliance pressure from customers/regulation
