# SciLens — Pharma Industrial Scoring Guide

## Scope
Pharmaceutical manufacturing, process chemistry, CMC, scale-up, GMP, formulation, API synthesis.

## TRL Calibration
| Paper evidence | TRL |
|---------------|-----|
| Synthetic route proposed, lab scale | 1–2 |
| Lab-scale synthesis with characterization | 3 |
| Process optimization, yields reported | 4 |
| Pilot-scale demonstration | 5–6 |
| Validation batch, GMP-compatible | 7 |
| Commercial-scale qualified | 8–9 |

## Evidence Quality Priority
Commercial-scale demonstration > pilot-scale + validation > lab-scale + scale-up model > theoretical

## Key Analysis Focus
- ICH compliance: Q8 (Pharmaceutical Development), Q9 (Quality Risk), Q10 (QMS)
- Yield percentages, cost of goods (COGS) signals
- Stability data (ICH Q1A) and impurity profiles (ICH Q3)
- Technology transfer feasibility
- GMP/GLP compliance signals

## Market Context Instructions
Industrial pharma market is driven by manufacturing contracts and API supply chain.
Reference trial/approval data as downstream demand signal for the API/formulation.

## Risk Factors to Prioritize
1. Scale-up failure from lab to pilot to commercial
2. Impurity control and regulatory acceptance
3. Raw material supply chain concentration
4. GMP compliance complexity for the process
5. IP protection for the process vs. product
